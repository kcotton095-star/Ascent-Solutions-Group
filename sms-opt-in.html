SMS call alerts — optional opt-in

Ascent Solutions Group answers after-hours calls for plumbing companies and sends the call details to the business owner. Alerts are delivered by email to every client. If you also want those same alerts as a text message, you can turn that on below.

Text messages are not required. You do not have to agree to SMS to buy, start, keep, or use the Ascent answering service, and agreeing to SMS is not a condition of any purchase or transaction. Clients who do not opt in receive their call alerts by email only, with no change to price or service.

What you're signing up for

  • Program name: Ascent Solutions Group — After-Hours Call Alerts
  • Who receives the texts: the business owner or on-call contact at a plumbing company that uses our answering service. We do not text the general public.
  • What we send: a summary of each after-hours call we answer on your behalf (caller name, callback number, service address, issue, urgency), plus occasional account and service notices.
  • How often: message frequency varies — roughly one message per after-hours call we answer for you.
  • Cost: Ascent does not charge for the texts. Message and data rates may apply from your carrier.
  • Stopping: reply STOP to any message to opt out. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at (854) 264-6928 / hello@ascentsolutionsgroup.org.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

How to stop texts

Reply STOP to any message from us. You'll get one confirmation message and then nothing further to that number. Your answering service keeps running exactly as before — opting out of SMS does not cancel, downgrade, or interrupt your service. To start again, reply START or email us.
